About the Office

The Office of the Sub Inspector of Schools, Adra Circle, plays a key role in supervising and supporting the effective implementation of educational policies, ensuring academic quality, and assisting school authorities in administrative matters for all schools under its jurisdiction in the Adra region. This includes academic monitoring, reporting, coordination of government schemes, and interaction with stakeholders.

Jurisdiction

The Sub Inspector of Schools serves all government and recognised aided/unaided schools within the Adra Circle, ensuring compliance with educational standards and providing outreach to enhance the quality of teaching and learning in the area.
