Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Office of the Sub Inspector of Schools, Adra Circle. This office functions under the School Education Department, Government of West Bengal, and is responsible for academic supervision, administrative coordination, and implementation of government educational policies in schools under its jurisdiction.

Vision

To ensure a transparent, inclusive, and effective educational administration that promotes quality teaching–learning practices and holistic development of students.

Key Functions

  • Inspection and academic supervision of schools
  • Monitoring of educational standards and compliance
  • Implementation of state and central government schemes
  • Guidance to school authorities and teaching staff
  • Support in administrative and academic matters

Notices & Announcements

Official notices, circulars, inspection schedules, and important announcements will be published here for the information of schools and stakeholders.
