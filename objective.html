Office of the Sub Inspector of Schools, Adra

School Education Department, Government of West Bengal

Objectives of the Office

The Office of the Sub Inspector of Schools, Adra Circle functions with the primary objective of strengthening the school education system through effective academic supervision, transparent administration, and faithful implementation of educational policies of the Government of West Bengal.

Primary Objectives

  • To ensure effective academic supervision of schools under the Adra Circle
  • To maintain and enhance the quality of teaching–learning processes
  • To monitor compliance with rules, regulations, and directives issued by the School Education Department
  • To promote inclusive and equitable access to education for all students
  • To support school authorities in academic and administrative improvement

Administrative Objectives

  • To assist in the implementation of government schemes and programmes related to school education
  • To facilitate coordination between schools and higher educational authorities
  • To ensure timely submission and verification of academic and administrative records
  • To support teachers in service-related and professional development matters

Commitment to Quality Education

This office remains committed to fostering a transparent, accountable, and student-centric educational environment. Through continuous monitoring, guidance, and collaboration with schools, the Sub Inspector of Schools, Adra Circle, strives to contribute meaningfully to the overall development of school education in the region.
